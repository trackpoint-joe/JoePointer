My Perspective

Tri-State's AI Opportunity

The Insight

Tri-State doesn't have a technology deficit. You have engineers, you have data, you have operational expertise. What's missing is the connective layer — someone who can translate AI capabilities into solutions that your teams actually adopt and use to make better decisions.

That's where 70% of AI initiatives fail. Not in the technology, but in the adoption. And that's where I've spent my career.

Three Immediate Opportunities

Based on our conversation and my research, these are the AI use cases I'd prioritize:

  1. Member Retention Intelligence — AI-driven early warning system monitoring board activity, rate sentiment, and exit signals across your member cooperatives. The pattern from departed members (United Power, LPEA) shows 12-24 months of visible signals before each exit. AI can flag those signals early enough to act.
  2. SPP Market Optimization — With RTO West now live, you have new market data flows. Price forecasting and dispatch optimization AI could improve purchasing decisions and reduce wholesale costs passed to members.
  3. Compliance Intelligence — An AI document assistant for NERC CIP standards, tariff documents, and regulatory filings. The fastest win in any organization — reducing the hours your compliance and legal teams spend searching for information.
