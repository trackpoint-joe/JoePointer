# Image Requirements for Portfolio

## Required Images

### 1. Professional Headshot (CRITICAL)
**Location:** Home page, top right or center
**Specs:**
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Size: 400x400px minimum (square)
- Background: Professional (solid color or subtle)
- Style: Business professional, approachable
- File name: `headshot.jpg`

**Why:** Increases trust by 25%, humanizes the portfolio

---

### 2. Case Study Hero Images (RECOMMENDED)

#### CareerSpark
**Concept:** Person looking at computer with confidence/clarity
**Alternative:** Abstract representation of AI + career growth
**File name:** `careerspark-hero.jpg`
**Size:** 1200x600px

#### Workplace Modernization
**Concept:** Modern office space or team collaboration
**Alternative:** Before/after office transformation
**File name:** `workplace-hero.jpg`
**Size:** 1200x600px

#### Revolver
**Concept:** Content flowing across multiple devices
**Alternative:** Abstract representation of knowledge architecture
**File name:** `revolver-hero.jpg`
**Size:** 1200x600px

---

## Professional Photography

**Recommended:** Hire professional for headshot
**Cost:** $150-300
**ROI:** Significantly increases credibility and response rates

**DIY Tips if needed:**
- Natural lighting (near window)
- Solid background (wall or backdrop)
- Business casual attire
- Slight smile, approachable
- Eye level camera angle
- High resolution (use portrait mode on phone)
